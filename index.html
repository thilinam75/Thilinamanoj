About

Hi, I'm Thilina Manoj Kumara — a passionate Software Engineer, Photographer, Designer, and Exporter from Sri Lanka. With a strong background in software development and a creative eye for design and photography, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision to every project I take on. I hold a Higher National Diploma in Computing and am currently completing my final year in Software Engineering at Esoft Metro Campus. I’ve gained hands-on experience through my internship at Exesmart Dynamics (Pvt) Ltd, working on real-world projects using technologies like C#, ASP.NET, VB.NET, SQL Server, and Crystal Reports.Ktupotha , Sri Lanka Outside the tech world, I run Studio Mano Arts, where I express my creativity through photography — capturing moments that matter. I’m also the founder of TM Creations, a CV and design service, and I explore Sri Lanka’s beauty through my YouTube channel The Travel Vlogger MANO. Whether I’m coding a web application, crafting visual content, or exploring local cultures, my goal is simple — to create meaningful experiences that leave a lasting impact.
